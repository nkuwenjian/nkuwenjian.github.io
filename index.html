Biography

I am currently a PhD candidate in the Institute of Robotics and Automatic Information System, Nankai University, supervised by Prof. Xuebo Zhang. Before that, I received the B.S. degree of Automation in the College of Computer and Control Engineering, Nankai University, in 2017.

My research focuses on mapping and motion planning for mobile robots.
