Biography

I received the B.S. degree in automation from Nankai University in June 2017, and the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering from Nankai University in June 2022, supervised by Prof. Xuebo Zhang. I am currently working as a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Xiaomi Technology.

My research focuses on behavioral reasoning, decision making, and motion planning for autonomous passenger vehicles.
