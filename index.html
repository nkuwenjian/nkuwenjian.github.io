Biography

I am currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in control science and engineering with the Institute of Robotics and Automatic Information System, Nankai University, supervised by Prof. Xuebo Zhang. Before that, I received the B.S. degree in automation from Nankai University, Tianjin, China, in 2017.

My research focuses on mapping and motion planning for mobile robots.
